Homeless in Austin has been a problem over the years.There are multiple people out in the streets day to day and have barely any shelter where they are at. These people are humans too and deserve a place to stay at. This website will help provide different resources for the people in need for an easier access to these materials. It will also bring awareness to the people that are uninformed about the topic in order to educate the people that want to know more about this topic.

Types of Homeless

There are different types of homeless some may be more intese than the others. The main three types of homeless are Chronically, Transitional, and Episodic each one of them is a different level of homeless

Chronically - This is a person which has experienced homeless multiple times. They sometimes have a health issue as well which makes them hard to get a job and a suitable living condition for themselves.

Transitional - This is the most common form of homeless. This is mostly younger people that need shelter. They often get help from homeless shelters and this could either benefit them or just make there condition worse.

Episodic - This is when indivduals experience homeless but some times they don't. They would trade between rehabs or some shelters to get some medical help but after they are done they go back to the streets.


Causes of Homeless

Lack of affordable housing.The cost of living in general has increased a significant amount over the course of the years.In the austin area to live comfortably you will need about $80,000 a year.

Lack of access of health services or for substance abuse. There is a massive amount of people that serve from a mental illness. The majority of the people of the streets have some sort of disease or illness tha unables them to be stable in an environment where there are people.

Employment factors there is a lot of unemployment. There is sometimes low wages that the jobs they give you for working but that doesn't give you enough to live in a well environment. Also if they have limited skills in working they have a limited amount of options where they are eligible to work at.
